About

In 2011 I graduated from Michigan State University with a degree in Media Arts Interactive Media. My focus had been on 3D animation and compositing footage, using the software Autodesk Maya, Pixologic Zbrush, and Adobe After Effects. As I found after graduating I realized I needed to cultivate an online presence, so I took classes at Oakland Community College to learn how to build websites. Upon completing these courses I realized I had enjoyed the process of designing, building, and presenting websites. Taking something that was just an idea and developing it into an interactive experience is facinating to me. Then in 2014 I studied responsive design at Grand Circus, a trainning institute in Detroit, Michigan. My long term goal is to develop and grow my skills into this field so I can cultivate unique experiences.
