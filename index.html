About

In 2011 I graduated from Michigan State University with a degree in Media Arts Interactive Media. My focus had been on 3D animation and compositing footage, using the software Autodesk Maya, Pixologic Zbrush, and Adobe After Effects. As I found after graduating I realized I needed to cultivate an online presence, so I took classes at Oakland Community College to learn how to build websites. Upon completing these courses I realized I had enjoyed the process of designing, building, and presenting websites. Taking something that was just an idea and developing it into an interactive experience. In 2014 I took another course in Building Dynamic Websites through Grand Circus, a trainning institute in Detroit, Michigan.
